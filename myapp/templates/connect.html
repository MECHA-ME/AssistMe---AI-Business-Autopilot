Connect on WhatsApp

Open a WhatsApp Web conversation with us and let Django capture the incoming chat in the admin portal.

Use international format, e.g. 15551234567 without spaces or + signs.

How it works

  • 1. Enter your number in international format.
  • 2. Click Open WhatsApp Web to start the chat.
  • 3. Messages you send from WhatsApp will be captured in Django via the webhook.
